The 1602 liquid crystal SDA pin is connected with the Magicbit extension plate SDA pin
The 1602 LCD SCL pin is connected to the Magicbit expansion plate SCL pin
The 1602 LCD GND pin is connected to the Magicbit extension plate GND pin
1602 LCD VCC pin is connected with Magicbit extension plate 5V or 3.3v pin (specific connection according to the actual situation)
